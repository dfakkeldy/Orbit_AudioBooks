This build: TokenDTW word-level alignment repair, richer EPUB rendering (bold/italic, images, links), photo bookmarks polish, hardened watch sync, Pomodoro timer, and alternate app icons.

PLEASE FOCUS ON

1) AUTO-ALIGNMENT (biggest ask)
• Add an ePub next to an audiobook and tap Auto-Align Chapters.
• Watch the tiers run (snap → drift detection → repair). Did chapters land in the right place?
• Find a misaligned paragraph, long-press → Align to Now. Does everything around it snap into shape?
• Report the book title + narrator + ePub source for any book that aligns badly — bad books are gold for us.

2) EPUB READER
• Do bold/italic text, images, and block quotes render correctly in the reader feed?
• Search for a phrase and tap a result — do text AND audio jump together?

3) PHOTO BOOKMARKS
• Attach a photo to a bookmark (library or camera). When playback passes it, does the player artwork switch to your photo — on phone, watch, and lock screen — then switch back?

4) APPLE WATCH
• Rearrange your button layout from the phone — does it appear on the wrist immediately?
• Leave the watch off overnight, then resume in the morning. Correct book, correct position, no phantom pause?
• Try the Pomodoro timer: set it, lower your wrist, wait for the alarm.

5) EVERYDAY PLAYBACK
• Pull out your headphones / aux mid-sentence — playback should pause, not blast the room.
• Pause for seconds / minutes / hours and resume — does Smart Rewind back up a sensible amount each time?
• Loop one chapter for a stretch — any hiccup at the boundary?

KNOWN THIS BUILD
• First auto-align run downloads the on-device speech model (~40 MB) and works the Neural Engine hard — plug in for your first full book.
• CarPlay is intentionally minimal (browse + transport).
• iCloud sync currently covers alignment anchors; full bookmark/flashcard sync is on the roadmap.
