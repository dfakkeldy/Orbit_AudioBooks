Echo: Audiobook Study Player

For Every Mind.

Most audiobook apps are built for passive listening. Echo is built for learning, retention, and brains that need a little extra flexibility. Whether you need an Anki-style flashcard system, true ePub text-alignment, or a custom drag-and-drop Apple Watch app, Echo turns your audiobooks into a true study tool.

THE ORIGIN STORY

The idea for Echo started behind the wheel of a mail delivery vehicle. Jumping in and out of a car thirty times a day makes it impossible to focus on complex non-fiction. Traditional apps constantly lost the playback spot, watch controls were too small to tap quickly, and retaining information with intermittent attention was a constant struggle.

Echo was built to fix all of that. Designed originally to accommodate AuDHD processing, its robust toolset is perfect for lifelong learners, commuters, and anyone who actually wants to absorb their books.

FEATURES BUILT FOR FOCUS

• Intermittent Attention Support — Smart rewind ensures you never lose context when you hit play after a pause. The longer you've been away, the further it rewinds.

• Chapter Looping — Put a single chapter on repeat until the concepts are fully absorbed. Loop between bookmarks for targeted review.

• Voice Memo Bookmarks — Instantly save your thoughts without fumbling with your phone. Perfect for driving, walking, or when your hands are full.

• Spaced Repetition (SRS) — Built-in Anki-style flashcard system using the SM-2 algorithm to help you memorize crucial facts, languages, or concepts permanently. Review on your phone or Apple Watch.

• True ePub Alignment — Seamlessly scroll through the text and view diagrams exactly when the audio reaches that section. On-device auto-alignment maps every paragraph to the narration using WhisperKit and CoreML.

• Pristine Speed Control — Listen at 1.25x (or faster) with zero pitch distortion. Speed suggestions adapt to your listening habits.

• Apple Watch Remote — A massive, user-configurable interface with up to 25 customizable buttons across 5 pages. Configure every action slot from the iPhone Settings app. Assign the Digital Crown to control volume or scrub playback.

ACCESSIBILITY & NEURODIVERSITY FIRST

Echo was designed from the ground up to support neurodivergent learning styles. The app icon — an infinity symbol (∞) in silver and gold — is a nod to the AuDHD community. The name "Echo" reflects how many neurodivergent brains work: ideas echoing between different modes of thinking, with text and audio reinforcing each other.

• Dyslexia-Optimized Fonts — Choose between research-backed Lexend (improved reading fluency) or OpenDyslexic (weighted letterforms to combat reversal).

• Hybrid Reading + Listening — Never forced to learn by audio alone. The synced ePub reader gives you text as an anchor while you listen.

OFFLINE ACCESS TIP
For the best experience, store your audiobooks in an "On My iPhone" or "On My iPad" folder. If you use iCloud Drive, long-press your audiobook folder in the Files app and choose "Keep Downloaded" to ensure your books are always available — even without an internet connection.
