Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. At Orbit Audiobooks, we have a few fundamental principles:

1. We Don't Collect Your Data

Orbit Audiobooks is designed to respect your privacy. All of your audiobook files, reading progress, voice memos, text notes, bookmarks, and flashcards are stored locally on your device or synced securely via your personal Apple iCloud account.

2. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics trackers, crash reporters, or advertising networks. We do not track what books you listen to, when you listen, or how you interact with the app. What happens in Orbit stays on your device.

3. Apple Health Data

If you choose to integrate Orbit with Apple Health (for example, via Apple Watch), this data is processed locally and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via our GitHub Issues page.
